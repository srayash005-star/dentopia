Dental equipment and technology

Dentopia uses modern dental devices to improve diagnosis, comfort, and treatment speed for every patient.

Digital X-Ray

Sharp imaging for faster diagnosis with lower radiation exposure.

Intraoral Camera

Real-time mouth photography helps you see exactly what the dentist sees.

Laser Dentistry

Gentle soft-tissue treatment with faster healing and less discomfort.

3D CBCT Scanner

Precise 3D scans for implants, surgery planning, and complex cases.

CAD/CAM System

Digital design and fabrication for same-day crowns and restorations.
